02 · The Solution

One system. Full ownership of the incident lifecycle.

MG-CRO correlates alerts, resolves low-risk incidents autonomously, and routes complex ones pre-diagnosed to the right team — eliminating ticket noise entirely.

01
Perception & Correlation
Event Intake & Root Cause Grouping
Ingests, enriches, groups and suppresses cascading alerts. Raw alert noise consolidated into one actionable root cause event — change conflicts blocked before they happen.
Alert dedupBlast radiusMaintenance gate
02
Decision & Risk Agent
Classification, Autonomy & Playbook Selection
Classifies risk, sets autonomy level, selects the best playbook. Every incident gets the right level of response — including alert variants never seen before.
Risk scoringRAG selectionAutonomy policy
03
Execution & Safety Agent
Dry-Run Gating & Autonomous Remediation
Dry-runs, executes, halts if unstable. Ansible never runs blindly. Low and medium risk incidents resolved end-to-end with no human involvement and no risk of worsening the incident.
Dry-run gateSyntax validateAuto-rollback
04
Verification & Approval Agent
Post-Check & Human-in-the-Loop Gateway
Confirms resolution, gates medium and high risk. Resolution confirmed by data not assumption. Engineers stay in control where it matters with a complete picture before touching anything.
Post-checkTeams cardOne-click approve
05
Reporting & Learning Agent
Audit, Escalation & Continuous Improvement
Logs, assigns, ranks, flags patterns. No ticket flooding, no blank escalations, no queue triage. Every record complete and auditable. The system improves with every incident.
